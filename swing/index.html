Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Swing! app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Nikita Lesnikov (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service.


Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
  • The time spent on the Application
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device
  • A unique anonymous identifier (Firebase Authentication UID) used to identify your leaderboard entry
  • Your chosen nickname (if you provide one) and your country (auto-detected or chosen by you)
  • Your game scores and gameplay progress, stored in Cloud Firestore for the public leaderboard
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information (collected when the Application crashes)

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.


The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your personal data. Some third-party services (e.g., AdMob) may use machine learning to personalize advertisements, as described in their respective privacy policies.


The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide important information, required notices and marketing promotions.


For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information (such as a nickname). This information will be retained by the Service Provider and used as described in this privacy policy.


Public Leaderboard

The Application includes a public leaderboard feature. When you play the game, your nickname, country, and score may be visible to other users of the Application. You can choose your nickname and country, or skip providing them. If you do not provide a nickname, an anonymous identifier (e.g., "Player1234") will be used. You can update or remove your leaderboard entry by contacting the Service Provider via email at lesnikov1@icloud.com.
